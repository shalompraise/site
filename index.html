FAQ's

What's the average cost for you to create an app?

The final price directly relates to the project scope, timeline and deliverables. As real project scope can't be determined wothout a formed strategy, the first first stage of the project is Design Strategy.

After this stage, together we'll have a clear overview of what we're building, what resources are required and how much time we'll need to complete the project. Once we have a clear picture of this, I'll be able to propose you the final price and deadline.

Based on my previous projects, clients typically come to me with more than $300 and less than $2,000 in their budget.

How long does it take you to deliver an app?

It depends on the project scope. But from previous experience, designing and building is in reference to the amount of features needed. A Small App (i.e Notes App) takes 3-4 weeks, Mid-size apps (i.e Recipe App) take 5-6 weeks and Big-size apps (i.e Food delivery) takes 8-12 weeks.

Do you build the apps all by yourself?

Depending on your need,scale and time of delivery, i take into account if i will need to collaborate with other developers through my links or have it worked out solo. Either case you will be involved in the whole process.

What can i expects in our first call?

Get to know each other and introductions, choose social platfform for communications and have an understanding of your great idea for the app.
